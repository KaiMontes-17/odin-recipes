Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a layered pasta dish with sauce, cheese, and fillings. It’s baked until rich, golden, and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Cook the filling: Brown ground beef with garlic, then stir in tomato sauce and herbs.
  2. Prepare layers: Boil lasagna noodles and mix ricotta with a bit of salt (and an egg if you like).
  3. Assemble: Layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella in a baking dish, repeating until full.
  4. Bake: Top with mozzarella and Parmesan, then bake at 375°F (190°C) for about 40 minutes until golden and bubbly.
