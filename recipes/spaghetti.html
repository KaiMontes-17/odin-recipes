Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is long, thin pasta often served with flavorful sauces. It’s simple, versatile, and widely loved.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Cook pasta: Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente, then drain.
  2. Make sauce: Sauté garlic (and onion if using) in olive oil, add tomato sauce and season with herbs and salt.
  3. Combine: Toss the cooked spaghetti with the simmered sauce.
  4. Serve: Top with Parmesan cheese (and meat or extras if desired).
