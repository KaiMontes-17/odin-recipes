Pizza

Description

Pizza is a flat dough topped with sauce, cheese, and toppings, then baked.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarela cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough by mixing and kneading ingredients, then roll it out.
  2. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  3. Add cheese and toppings of your choice.
  4. Bake in a hot oven until the crust is golden and cheese is melted.
